About Lilleå

Lilleå, as suggested by its name, is a little river that runs off the Gudenå. It offers some of the best sea trout fishing in all of Denmark but you can also catch a variety of other fish such as the flatfish or pike.

Fish aren’t the only thing in store along the river either as you can often catch a glimpse of a beautiful kingfisher or an adorable weasel. In order to fish here, you will need a national angling license and a Hadsten fishing association membership or day pass.

Fishing is allowed at all hours between 01/03 to 31/10 but please do not bring your dog with you!
