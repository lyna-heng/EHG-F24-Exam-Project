Vadsted Storsø

About Vadsted Storesø

Vadsted Storsø is the larger of two lakes near Hammel and is just a short walk away from the parking area.
Here you can fish for pike (conservation period 15/03-30/04) and spend a peaceful moment out in nature.
You can also make use of the rain shelter and space for a campfire fitted out with benches.
